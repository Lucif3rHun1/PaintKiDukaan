# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Paint shop owners and staff use PaintKiDukaan at the counter and back office while selling paint, receiving stock, managing parties, closing the day, and checking operational health. Users need fast, familiar workflows that preserve inventory and money accuracy under real shop pressure.

## Product Purpose

PaintKiDukaan is a secure desktop inventory and billing system for paint shops. It exists to keep POS billing, inward stock movement, customer/vendor balances, reports, backup, and health checks reliable in one local-first Tauri app. Success means a shop can trust every rupee, stock quantity, and session state without leaving the selling workflow.

## Brand Personality

Practical, trustworthy, focused. The interface should feel like a disciplined trade counter tool: calm enough for long daily use, clear enough for cashier speed, and sturdy enough for owner-level financial decisions.

## Anti-references

Do not make the app look like a generic SaaS landing page, glassy dashboard, playful consumer app, or decorative analytics product. Avoid ornamental gradients, oversized cards, hero metrics, excessive shadows, and money displays that obscure the backend paise contract.

## Design Principles

1. Money is sacred: paise is the wire format, rupees are only an edge display/input concern.
2. Earn familiarity: use consistent product UI affordances over novelty.
3. Preserve task flow: navigation, actions, and state feedback should reduce clicks and context switching.
4. Separate shell from work: keep the dark app chrome distinct from light, dense operational surfaces.
5. Teach empty and error states: when data is missing or blocked, explain the next useful action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for all text and controls. Use focus-visible states, disabled states, tabular numbers for money, keyboard-accessible menus/dialogs, and reduced-motion-safe transitions. Color must not be the only signal for status or destructive actions.
